Take Action

How can you help the animals on this planet? More obviously, please do not engage in animal cruelty. Please report any cases of animal cruelty as soon as possible. Furthermore, sign petitions or donate to trusted organizations or campaigns. Additionally, help clean up and maintain animal habitats and help stray animals by taking them to animal shelters. Moreover, advocate the removal of torture animal videos from YouTube and other media sources. Below, you’ll find a couple of petitions to help you take action. If you wish to see more, go to the Our Mission page or visit Lady Freethinker.
